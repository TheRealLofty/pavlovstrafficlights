Class Traffic Light

Green can move to yellow. Yellow can move back to green. Red is locked. Moves and consequences are time-stamped for export.

Green

Ready to learn

Yellow

Warning / chance to move back up

Red

Locked on red — max: Buddy Class, then R & R
